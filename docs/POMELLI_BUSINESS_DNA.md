# Google Pomelli: Business DNA Profile for QAScope

Copy and paste the sections below directly into the corresponding input fields in your **Google Pomelli (Pomelo)** branding and setup dashboard. This seeds Pomelli's generative model with the exact visual identities, positioning limits, and tonal parameters of QAScope.

---

## 1. Company Name & One-Liner
* **Company Name:** `QAScope`
* **Wordmark Rules:** `QA` in teal, `Scope` in zinc/white. Strictly sans-serif. No spaces.
* **One-Liner Hook:** `AI QA copilot for support teams. Stop sampling. Start scoring 100% of your customer conversations.`

---

## 2. Core Positioning & Mission
* **What QAScope Is:** A modern, high-throughput, and cost-effective conversation auditing platform designed for BPOs and customer support campaigns. It automates conversation grading, identifies compliance violations, and generates structured coaching summaries for support agents.
* **The Brand Promise:** 100% conversation coverage, zero backlog, and massive cost efficiency through Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) pricing.
* **What We Avoid (Negative Guidelines):**
  - We do *not* sell "AI hype" or generic "robot replacements."
  - We do *not* claim that AI replaces human reviewers.
  - We do *not* use images of robots, futuristic metallic nodes, or brains.
  - We sell *operational excellence, compliance security, and cost-efficiency.*

---

## 3. Brand Voice and Tone (Input to Pomelli Copywriter)
* **Tone Profile:** Plain-speaking, highly operational, authoritative yet accessible, ex-operator credibility, numbers-driven.
* **Key Voice Directives:**
  - **Be direct:** Speak like a former BPO Operations Director who got tired of legacy processes. Use operational shorthand (e.g., SLA, rubric, calibration, compliance escape, QA agent, coaching loop).
  - **Avoid corporate fluff:** Never use buzzwords like *synergize, revolutionize, unlock potential, transformational journey, next-gen paradigm.*
  - **Be transparent:** Always be honest about how the AI works. Frame it as a *force-multiplier for human QA managers*, not an automated black box.
  - **Quantified facts:** Always lead with numbers. For example: *"Indian BPOs sample 5% of calls. That means 95% of compliance risks are invisible. QAScope audits 100% in 15 minutes."*

## 4. Visual Identity & Brand Styling (Input to Pomelli Visual Designer)
Input these exact parameters into Pomelli's visual DNA generators to ensure all generated ads, carousels, and landing page wireframes match the production app.

### A. Color Palette (Hex Codes)
* **Primary Background:** `#09090B` (Zinc-950 - sleek, premium dark space)
* **Secondary Surface:** `#18181B` (Zinc-900 - card surfaces and borders)
* **Accent Brand Light:** `#14B8A6` (Teal-500 - primary visual focus, active states, icons, glow accents)
* **Accent Brand Dark:** `#134E4A` (Teal-900 - deep card fills, glow gradients)
* **Neutral Text Primary:** `#F4F4F5` (Zinc-100 - headings, high legibility body)
* **Neutral Text Muted:** `#A1A1AA` (Zinc-400 - descriptions and sub-labels)
* **Danger (Compliance Fail):** `#DC2626` (Red-600)
* **Warning (Needs Review):** `#F59E0B` (Amber-500)
* **Success (Verified Audit):** `#059669` (Emerald-600)

### B. Typography Style
* **Primary Header Font:** `Geist` or `Inter` (Bold weight, tracking-tight, high-contrast white text)
* **Body Text Font:** `Geist` or `Inter` (Medium/Light weight, high readability)
* **Monospace Font (Code/Transcripts):** `Geist Mono` or `Consolas` (Used for displaying ticket IDs, API keys, and raw transcripts in graphics)

### C. Creative Asset Style (Image / Banner Prompts)
When Pomelli requests graphics, use this style profile:
* **Style:** High-end developer-centric dark mode, minimalist, glassmorphic card overlays, neon-glow borders.
* **Graphic Fills:** Use realistic screenshots of data dashboards, line charts, clean database list structures, and highlighted chat logs.
* **Strict Exclusions:** No vector illustrations of generic smiling people, no cartoon clip-arts, no glowing robot brains, no abstract colorful shapes. Everything must look like a high-performance developer dashboard or operator interface.
