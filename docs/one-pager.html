The 5% problem

Most QA teams review 5% of conversations. The other 95% is where compliance fails and coaching opportunities hide.

QAScope scores every customer interaction against your rubric, flags compliance violations and low-scoring agents, and gives team leads a prioritized review queue with SLA-tracked auto-approve. Built by an ex-BPO operator for SMB BPOs in India.

How it works

1Upload conversations

Upload a CSV from any source — Genesys, Five9, Zendesk, Excel exports. Auto-detect maps your columns to QAScope fields. Voice, email, chat all supported.

2AI scores against your rubric

Every conversation graded on 7 weighted criteria + your project-specific fatal rules (e.g. PII verification, RBI compliance). Confidence scoring + a coaching note per agent.

3Two-tier human review

Anything flagged for compliance or low confidence lands in a review queue with a configurable SLA. Team lead agrees or escalates. Auto-approves if not actioned in time.

What you get

Manager dashboard

30-day KPIs, top/bottom agent leaderboards, by-team rollup, by-channel split, status mix, appeal correction rate.

Saved report templates

Build a custom report once via plain English (“agents below 70% in Mumbai this week”). Re-run it forever, free.

Bring your own LLM

Works with OpenAI, OpenRouter, Together AI, Groq, Azure, or any OpenAI-API-compatible endpoint. Your key, your tokens, transparent cost in the dashboard.

Multi-tenant + RLS

Postgres row-level security at the database. Your data is invisible to every other workspace.
