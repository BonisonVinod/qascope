## LEFT COLUMN — How it works (3 steps)

### 1. Upload conversations

Upload a CSV from any source — Genesys, Five9, Zendesk, Excel exports. Auto-detect maps your columns to QAScope fields. Voice, email, chat all supported.

### 2. AI scores against your rubric

Every conversation graded on 7 weighted criteria + your project-specific fatal rules (compliance violations like "missed PII verification" or "promised refund without authorization"). Confidence scoring + a coaching note per agent.

### 3. Two-tier human review

Anything flagged for compliance or low confidence lands in a review queue with a configurable SLA. Team lead agrees or escalates to a senior reviewer. Auto-approves if nobody actions within SLA — no items rot.

## DESIGN NOTES (for whoever turns this into a PDF)

- **Font:** Inter or Geist for body; bolder weight for headings
- **Colors:** zinc/charcoal primary text, single accent (emerald `#10B981` or your brand color) for highlights and the price column
- **Whitespace generously** — this is a one-pager, not a brochure. Don't cram.
- **Logo + tagline at top** should occupy real estate; that's the first thing they see
- **Pricing table is the most important asset** — make it visually anchor the page
- **Footer CTA should feel like a button**, not a paragraph
- **No stock photography of "diverse team smiling at laptop."** Either real screenshots of QAScope or no images at all.

If you want one screenshot, use the **dashboard** with realistic-looking numbers — that's what most resonates with a QA buyer.
