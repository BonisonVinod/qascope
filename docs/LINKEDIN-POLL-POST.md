## ⏰ How to Schedule for Maximum Reach

LinkedIn's algorithm heavily favors posts that get early engagement. Since you are targeting BPO operators and leaders (mostly in India based on your previous preferences):

1. **Best Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday. (Avoid Mondays when people are putting out fires, and Fridays when they check out early).
2. **Best Times (IST):** 
   - **Morning Commute/Coffee:** 8:30 AM – 9:30 AM
   - **Post-Lunch Dip:** 12:30 PM – 1:30 PM
3. **How to Schedule Natively on LinkedIn:**
   - Go to your LinkedIn home feed and click "Start a post."
   - Click the **Poll icon** (the little bar chart) at the bottom.
   - Fill in your question and options. Click Done.
   - Paste the post copy above.
   - Click the **Clock icon** (Schedule) next to the "Post" button.
   - Select your ideal date and time (e.g., Wed at 9:00 AM).
   - Click "Next", then "Schedule."
